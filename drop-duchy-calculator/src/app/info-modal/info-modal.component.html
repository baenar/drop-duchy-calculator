Drop Duchy Calculator 🏰

Simple app helping maximize your score by calculating best unit order before battle in the game Drop Duchy.

Rules of Combat ⚔️
The goal is to arrange all units on the map in order, that gives best possible score for the player, by following rules below:
- 🗡️ Sword is super effective against 🪓 Axe
- 🪓 Axe is super effective against 🏹 Arrows
- 🏹 Arrows is super effective against 🗡️ Sword
- 💀 Boss is a strong enemy unit, that doesn't have a weakness


You can use this app to calculate the best order to send your units into battle. Just insert the units you have, then click the Calculate button. The app will suggest the best possible sequence.
